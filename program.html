  • Microsoft Suite

    From students to the C-Suite, Microsoft Office is the gold standard of productivity software. Today, 1.2 billion people across 140 countries and 107 languages use the productivity suite. Despite so many of us using Office programs in our daily lives, they can be a challenge to master without the right Microsoft Office Suite training. To this end, Delightsome Educational Institute (DEI) will help you learn the ins and outs of the widely-used software trio Excel, Word, and PowerPoint, as well as the other popular productivity programs Access, Outlook, Project and Publisher.
    • Who is this Microsoft Office training for? This Microsoft Office training is suited for anyone who wants to improve their skills and master Microsoft Office. You can choose which Microsoft Office courses to take based on your skill level and interests. If you are a beginner, or looking for a Microsoft Office refresher course, we are the ideal place to be. Even if you have some prior knowledge and want to further your skills, jump right into intermediate or advanced lessons, we are fully positioned to help you accomplish this.
    • What Microsoft Office courses are included? At Delightsome Educational Institute (DEI), you will learn 9 Microsoft Office Suite courses to enhance your skills and boost your productivity.
    The 9 Microsoft Office Suite are:
    • Microsoft Word The Basic and Advanced Word courses will take you from the basics – creating, saving, and sharing documents – to more advanced tasks, like tracking changes, line spacing, automating a table of contents, and more!
    • Microsoft Excel The Basic and Advanced Excel courses will take you from novice to Excel ninja. Master the essential skills you need to save time, be more productive, and earn a reputation as the office spreadsheet whiz. In the Basic course, you will learn essential functions and formulas, sorting, filtering and creating charts.
    • Microsoft PowerPoint In this course you will learn how to create dazzling presentations sure to impress your peers and superiors. You will learn how to use themes and layouts, add audio visual components to your presentation, add animations, print, reuse and copy slides plus more.
    • Microsoft Outlook If you subscribe to the notion that “with organization comes empowerment”, this course is perfect for you. The lessons equip you with tips and tricks for sending and organizing email, reducing inbox clutter, plus how to manage your calendar, contacts and tasks to maximize efficiency.
    • Microsoft Access Ideal if you have an interest in automation but don’t have a background in coding. The course takes you through VBA in incremental steps that are easy to understand and quick to implement. The practical classes teach you how to design tables and build relationships, create forms and reports, make simple and complex queries and created calculated fields, and more!
    • Microsoft Publisher Turn your desktop into a mini publishing house with Microsoft Publisher. Cheaper and easier to use than most publishing tools, this software is ideal for those with business publishing needs. In this course you will learn how to create print-ready business cards, brochures, posters, and more!

Becoming a web designer allows you to use your creativity, following briefs and putting your own individual spin and style on them. At Delightsome Educational Institute (DEI), we develop your competencies in website development and design and equip you with the critical skills that you need to thrive and succeed in the 21st century marketplace.     • Practical Web Development Training

    What makes Delightsome Educational Institute (DEI) web development training programme special?
    • Build Real Websites

    • Experienced Trainers
